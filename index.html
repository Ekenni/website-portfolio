About Me

I graduated from Iowa Western with an A.A.S. in Web Design and Development and an A.A.S. in Web Applications and Computer Programming. I joined Sandhills Global as an Interactive Designer and grew into a front-end-focused role where design and development naturally met.

Over the past four years, I've built and shipped more than 200 websites, designed front-end sites for brands like Dynapac, Daimler, and Volvo, and helped create a used-equipment experience that moved from mockup to production.

What I enjoy most is taking a thoughtful idea and turning it into something clear, useful, and genuinely well-crafted across the browser.
