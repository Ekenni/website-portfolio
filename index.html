About Me

I graduated from Iowa Western with an A.A.S. in Web Design and Development and an A.A.S. in Web Applications and Computer Programming. I interned at Sandhills Global while in school and got hired full-time as an Interactive Designer before I even walked the stage.

Four years later, I've designed and built over 200 websites, created mockups for brands like Dynapac, Daimler, and Volvo, and built a full used-construction-equipment site for Volvo. I also rebuilt our new-hire training documentation from the ground up and have personally trained 7 new designers.

I love using my creative side to design custom, user-friendly websites, but honestly? The best part is watching a design go from a flat mockup to something people can actually click around in.
